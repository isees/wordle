Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our One Piece Character Guessing Game ("the Game").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Game progress data (guesses, completion status, etc.)
  • A randomly generated user ID stored on your device
  • Game preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Maintain your gameplay statistics
  • Improve the game based on user patterns
  • Maintain and enhance the functionality of the Game

Local Storage

The Game primarily stores information locally on your device using:

  • Browser's localStorage
  • IndexedDB for larger data storage

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly stated.

Data Retention

Your game data is stored on your device until you:

  • Clear your browser data
  • Explicitly request data deletion through game settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your game data from your device
  • Request information about what data is stored

Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
